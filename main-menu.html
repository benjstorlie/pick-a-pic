Pick-a-Pick

Across the globe, we are presented with thousands of languages, from the billions who want to be heard.

But among the crowd, you will be met with those who have the acquisition to speak a refined language of their own; one that many struggle to understand, but one that certainly needs to be heard.

With this on-the-fly web application, we yearn to break the language barrier of communication between any and everyone. Whether they were born with a disability, or are putting forth effort in an attempt to understand one another.

Through the utilization of images, our web application will convey exactly what the user needs; in order to bridge communication between all individuals.

So cross the gap and connect.
